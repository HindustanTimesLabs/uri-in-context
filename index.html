Most of Kashmir’s Militancy is in Kupwara District
Militant attacks per district, 2010-2016

The attack on Uri took place in Baramulla District. Since January, 2010, 20 attacks by militants or terrorists have taken place in the district, the second most of all districts in the state.

Kupwara District, to the north-west of Baramulla, has borne the brunt of the militancy in Kashmir, having seen 56 attacks since 2010.
